Data Science Job Listing Australia

Exploring job oppurtunities for data scientists between 2019 - 2021 from seek.com.au

Which States Provide the Most Job Oppurtunities?

Data Science is an extremely promising field in Australia. The field is continuing to grow thanks to rise of big data, artificial intelligence and machine learning.

As an aspiring Data Scientist, you may be wandering where the majority of jobs are based. This may help you understand the oppurtunities avaialbale close to you, and to make the decision if relocating may be necessary.

Although there are a couple oppurtunities in other states, it is evident that the majority of oppurtunities are avaiable in Victoria and New South Wales. However, the job oppurtunities are not distributed evenly through out the state. They are infact mostly within the capital cities of Melbourne and Sydney.

How has the Demand for Data Scientists Changed?

As the "Sexiest Job of the 21st Century" according to the Harvard Business Review it is unsurprising that the demand for Data Scientist is rapdily expanding.

However, we can see a major drop in the number of job listings in 2020. This was caused by the COVID-19 gloabal pandemic which significantly affected the Australian Economy, resulting in a loss of job as well as fewer available oppurtunities.

With Australia's vaccination program kicking off in February 2021, we can see the job demand for Data Scientists rise rapidly in order to aid Australia with its economic recovery. The data collected upto the end of June makes the trend for increasing demand evident.

Which Fields have Available Oppurtunities?

Data Science is an extremely multi disciplinary. Every field has a lot of data that can be analyzed to discover significant insights. Therefore, there is a high demand for data scientists in fields ranging from Banking and Financial Services to Consulting and Strategy.

We can see that ICT has the largest percentage of the total jobs. However the ICT field in itself is exceptionally diverse. Let's dive in a little deeper to understand what type of jobs are offered in ICT.

What are the Job Oppurtunities in the ICT Field?

ICT offers a range of jobs types, that vary from Business/System Analysts to Software Engineers. This allows candidates to choose oppurtunities that best align with their interests and career goals.

Mathematics, Statistics and Information Sciences has the greatest percentage of ICT jobs. This is expected due to the increase in demands for data modelling and forecasting, which has been made possible with the vast amount of data which is now readily available.
